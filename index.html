Flick – Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Flick is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your personal data is handled when using the Flick mobile app.

2. No Data Collection

Flick does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data externally. All data you create (mood entries, voice notes, photos, and comments) is saved only on your device.

3. Data Control

You control your data. Uninstalling the app or deleting its data removes all stored information permanently. We cannot retrieve deleted data.

4. Permissions

The app may request access to:

  • Camera: for adding photos to mood entries
  • Microphone: for recording voice notes
  • Storage: for saving media locally
  • Notifications: for reminders or mood prompts

These permissions are only used for app functionality and never for data collection or sharing.

5. Third-Party Services

Flick does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud storage. Your data stays fully on your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

Flick is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app and take effect immediately.
